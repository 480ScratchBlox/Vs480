Vs. 480 is a reskin based off Vs. Dave and Bambi that not only contains most of the songs from the base mod, but it's own unique content as well. That being said, Vs. 480 is similar by Vs. Dave and Bambi, but features new characters, mechanics, and story. Since it's first release, the reskin has made it easy to modify or change. You can both add and delete as many features as your heart tells you to.

So what are you waiting for? Expirience it yourself here.
